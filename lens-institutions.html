Lens: Institutions & Governance

Institutions become brittle when limits are ignored in the name of efficiency.

Collapse is often delayed: the system looks stable while trust and redundancy drain away.

Signs

  • Rigidity
  • Loss of buffers and redundancy
  • Risk accumulating in the background
  • Short-term wins at long-term cost
  • Trust erosion
CD interpretation: resilience is being converted into short-term output.

Governance moves

  • Govern pace, not just performance
  • Protect recovery (maintenance, staffing, trust)
  • Set capacity budgets
  • Measure leading indicators
  • Avoid crisis theatre
CD avoids blame narratives. It treats brittleness as a design outcome.
CD focuses on capacity, limits, and recovery — not blame, optimisation, or spectacle.
