How to use CD responsibly

CD supports calm, early redesign — not judgement.

Use CD to ask: where is load exceeding recovery? Where are limits being ignored? What needs redesign?

Good for

  • Early diagnosis
  • Pacing decisions
  • Design reflection
  • Safeguarding recovery

Not for

  • Predicting collapse dates
  • Blaming individuals
  • Fear or spectacle
  • Instant “healing” theatre
If CD is being used to frighten, shame, or “prove” a predetermined conclusion, it is being misused.
CD focuses on capacity, limits, and recovery — not blame, optimisation, or spectacle.
