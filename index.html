Understanding systems before they break

Most systems don’t fail loudly.
They fail quietly — while still appearing to work.

Consilient Design (CD) helps people notice clinical depletion: when a system keeps functioning while losing its ability to adapt or recover. CD is a diagnostic and design framework for staying within limits — without blame, panic, or spectacle.
