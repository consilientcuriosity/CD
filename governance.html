Governance & ethics

CD is constrained on purpose — because spectacle changes meaning.

These constraints are ethical commitments: care, limits, and responsibility.

Constraints

  • No explosions / shatter
  • No crack crawling
  • No instant healing visuals
  • Slow pacing
Claim: depleted systems deserve calm attention and redesign — not crisis theatre.

Ethics

  • Care of Living Systems
  • Care of Human Systems
  • Balance of Distribution and Limits
CD is intended to be safe and legible in public and school contexts.
CD focuses on capacity, limits, and recovery — not blame, optimisation, or spectacle.
