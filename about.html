About

CD is designed to travel across contexts without losing meaning.

This site is the public interpretation layer. Technical presets and versioning belong in a repository. Decision memory belongs in stewardship records.

Animation scripts (handoff)

  • Story: healthy → warning window → depletion → brittle state
  • Invariant: crack locations stay fixed
  • Signal: widening / thinning / patchiness
  • No spectacle: avoid dramatic collapse aesthetics
  • Accessibility: respect reduced motion; provide still diagrams
CD focuses on capacity, limits, and recovery — not blame, optimisation, or spectacle.
