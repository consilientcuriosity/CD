Lens: Ecological

Ecosystems fail quietly through lost recovery capacity, not sudden catastrophe.

Degradation becomes patchy; recovery slows; fragmentation blocks return.

Signs

  • Patchy degradation
  • Ecological memory (legacy impacts)
  • Longer recovery after stress
  • Fragmentation
  • Phase shifts after “one more” event
CD interpretation: function may continue while adaptive capacity drains.

Design moves

  • Protect recovery windows
  • Reduce repeated low-level stress
  • Restore connectivity
  • Respect damaged places need different care
  • Measure recovery time and patchiness
CD rejects catastrophe aesthetics. It builds the case for earlier, quieter intervention.
CD focuses on capacity, limits, and recovery — not blame, optimisation, or spectacle.
